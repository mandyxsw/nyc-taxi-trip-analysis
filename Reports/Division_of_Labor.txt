Division of Labour

The project was completed through a structured division of responsibilities across different phases of the data science pipeline.

The Data Retrieval and Data Wrangling/Cleaning phase was completed by Pegah Aghili (40113508). Responsibilities included identifying and documenting the dataset source, retrieving and loading the dataset into the working environment, storing the raw data in its original format, performing an initial data audit, checking for missing values, duplicates, and inconsistencies, cleaning and preprocessing the dataset, and constructing a reproducible data wrangling pipeline. Exploratory diagnostics were also supported using tools such as quantDDA() and vizDDA().

The Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and Baseline Modeling phase was completed by Shengwen Xu (40023262). Responsibilities included performing summary statistics and distribution analysis, creating univariate and bivariate visualizations, conducting correlation analysis, formulating research questions, and designing and implementing a baseline linear regression model. This phase also involved performing a train/validation/test split and evaluating model performance using appropriate metrics.

The Unsupervised Learning, Feature Engineering, and Interpretation phase was completed by Mandy. Responsibilities included engineering new features (including time-based and domain-specific variables), preprocessing data for clustering through scaling and transformation, applying KMeans clustering, evaluating clustering performance using multiple metrics (inertia, silhouette score, Calinski-Harabasz, and Davies-Bouldin), applying Principal Component Analysis (PCA) for dimensionality reduction and visualization, and interpreting clustering results to identify meaningful patterns in trip behavior.

The Advanced Supervised Learning and Final Modeling phase was completed by Pegah Aghili (40113508). Responsibilities included designing and implementing the final supervised learning pipeline, performing additional feature engineering, implementing and comparing multiple models (such as Random Forest and XGBoost), conducting hyperparameter tuning using RandomizedSearchCV, evaluating model performance using metrics including MAE, MSE, RMSE, and R², analyzing model outputs (including residuals and feature importance), and performing robustness analysis across multiple random seeds.
